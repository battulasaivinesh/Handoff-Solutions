GPSTT

GPS TRACKING DEVICE
This is low power long range wireless GPS tracking device with accelerometer
Features
  • Live Tracking
  • Tracking History
  • Multi Vehicle View On Map
  • Dedicated Mobile And Desktop Application
  • Near By Petrol Pumps
  • Toll Road Fees and Indication
  • Call Driver Feature
  • Route Management(route to be taken, estimated time)
  • Geo Fence
  • Progress Bar of Every Truck in Desktop Application
  • Fuel Level Indicator
  • Customized Address System
  • App Notifications Alert, Mail And SMS Alerts:
    • Battery Disconnect
    • Abnormal truck movements
    • Over speed Alerts
    • Geo Fence In and Out
    • Arrival and Departure
  • Reports
    • Tracking History report
