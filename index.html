OUR SERVICES

HandsOff Solutions Internet of Things service enables organizations to transform business needs into competitive differentiators by delivering innovative IoT powered solutions. From integrating the right sensors and deriving inspired insights to choosing the best-fit platform, we provide comprehensive IoT services to our clients. We empower organizations to:
  • Measure process parameters e.g. temperature, humidity, pressure, fuel level, density, viscosity, etc.
  • Measure equipment health parameters e.g. temperature, vibration, speed, output, door status, breakdown, etc
  • Ensure preventive and predictive “Reliability Centric Maintenance” of equipment and plant.
  • Monitor, control and forecast production parameters e.g. quality, throughput, etc.
  • Detect safety threats e.g. level of hazardous gases or fluids, leaks, water pressure in fire hose tanks, etc..
OUR OFFERINGS

WHTT Sensor


Wireless humidity and temperature sensor
The WHTT Indoor Sensor is a low-power, long range, wireless sensor that provides 0.5 °C accurate temperature and 2 % RH humidity monitoring at every location. Our smart sensors make it possible to monitor 24/7 for up to 10 years without the need for maintenance.
W4TT


Wireless Temperature Transmitter
Model WTT is state of the art low-power, long range, wireless transmitter to measure and transmit Temperature with high accuracy. Up to 4 PT-100 can be connected.
QADC


Quad Channel Wireless device for transmitting 4-20mA Analog Signal
